The Car and Its Operation

What must be done before
starting the Car? Answer No. 1

Before trying to start the car, fill the radiator (by removing cap at top) with clean
fresh water. If perfectly clean water cannot be obtained it is advisable to strain
it through muslin or other similar material to prevent foreign matter from getting
in and obstructing the small tubes of the radiator. The system will hold approximately
three gallons. It is important that the car should not be run under its
own power unless the water circulating system has been filled. Pour in the water
until you are sure that both radiator and cylinder water jackets are full. The
water will run out of the overflow pipe onto the ground when the entire water
system has been filled.
