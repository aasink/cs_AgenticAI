Question 4: What oil should I use in a Model T engine?

WITHOUT RAG (model's own knowledge):
----------------------------------------
The correct answer is 30W-40. This type of motor oil has the right viscosity for the Model T's engine, which was designed to run on lower viscosity oils.

The Model T Ford, introduced in 1908, was one of the first automobiles to be mass-produced and widely available to the public. It featured an inline-six engine that required specific types of lubricants to function properly. One crucial aspect of maintaining the Model T's engine was ensuring it ran smoothly with the appropriate motor oil.

Motor oil serves several important functions in an internal combustion engine:

1. **Lubrication**: Motor oil acts as a barrier between moving parts, reducing friction and wear.
2. **Cooling**: Oil helps dissipate heat generated by the engine during operation.
3. **Cleaning**: It removes contaminants from the engine components.
4. **Sealing**: Some oils have additives that help prevent leaks and seal joints.

For the Model T, using the wrong type of motor oil could lead to serious issues such as overheating, increased wear on parts, or even damage to the engine itself. Therefore, selecting the right oil is essential for keeping your vintage car running efficiently.

### Why 30W-40?
A 30W-40 motor oil is specifically formulated for engines like those found in early cars, including the Model T. Here’s why it’s ideal:

- **Viscosity Range**: A 30W-40 oil provides a balance between low-temperature fluidity (the ability to flow easily at cold temperatures) and high-temperature stability (resistance to breakdown when heated). This makes it suitable for the Model T's engine, which typically operates under varying conditions but generally requires a moderate viscosity.
  
- **Compatibility**: These oils are known to perform well with older vehicles due to their compatibility with the materials used in these engines. They do not contain modern additives that might interfere with the original design of the engine.

- **Cost-effectiveness**: While higher-quality oils may offer better performance over time, 30W-40 oil is often more affordable and sufficient for most automotive needs without requiring frequent changes.

In summary, using 30W-40 motor oil for a Model T Ford ensures that its engine receives the proper lubrication needed to operate efficiently and safely. Always check the manufacturer’s recommendations for the best oil grade suited to your vehicle’s specific requirements.
