  downward, the ring again shifts to the top of the groove and the oil
  is deposited on the cylinder wall at a point above the top ring, and
  therefore, cannot be carried back with the piston on its down stroke.
  It will b e seen that a large amount of oil can be pumped into the
  combustion chamber in this manner.
